A website was created from a series of stories. The excerpt "In praise of shadows" by Jun'ichiro Tanizaki was chosen to depict light and dark in Japanese temples. To match the architecture, a cube-like text type was created with dark blues and orange hues inspired by the sunset color. This is compatible with all devices.
