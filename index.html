Elastic Collections

A website was created from a series of stories. The excerpt "In praise of shadows" by Jun'ichiro Tanizaki was chosen to depict light and dark in Japanese temples. To match the architecture, a cube-like text type was created with dark blues and orange hues inspired by the sunset color. This is compatible with all devices.

'Elastic collections' is designed to present a dataset of 25-50 items. The above website provides users with the opportunity to learn and understand "Morse Code" Letters of the alphabet have been broken down into sounds and are further divided by dots and dashes. Each alphabet has been provided with a mnemonic device to enhance their memorisation skills.
